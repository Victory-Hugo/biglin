摘要

mtDNA-Server: next-generation sequencing data analysis of human mitochondrial DNA in the cloud

“While several pipelines for analyzing heteroplasmies exist, issues in usability, accuracy of results and interpreting final data limit their usage.” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 64) 虽然存在多种分析异质性的方法，但可用性、结果准确性和最终数据解释方面的问题限制了它们的使用。

“mtDNAServer, a scalable web server for the analysis of mtDNA studies of any size with a special focus on usability as well as reliable identification and quantification of heteroplasmic variants.” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 64) mtDNAServer，一个可扩展的网络服务器，用于分析任何规模的 mtDNA 研究，特别关注可用性以及异质变异的可靠识别和量化。

“The mtDNA-Server workflow includes parallel read alignment, heteroplasmy detection, artefact or contamination identification, variant annotation as well as several quality control metrics, often neglected in current mtDNA NGS studies.” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 64) mtDNA-Server 工作流程包括并行读取比对、异质性检测、伪影或污染识别、变异注释以及一些质量控制指标，这些指标在当前 mtDNA NGS 研究中经常被忽视。

“Our evaluation data shows that mtDNAServer detects heteroplasmies and artificial recombinations down to the 1% level with perfect specificity and outperforms existing approaches regarding sensitivity.” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 64) 我们的评估数据显示，mtDNAServer 可检测低至 1% 水平的异质性和人工重组，具有完美的特异性，并且在灵敏度方面优于现有方法。

“mtDNA-Server is currently able to analyze the 1000G Phase 3 data (n= 2,504) in less than 5 h and is freely accessible” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 64) mtDNA-Server 目前能够在不到 5 小时内分析 1000G 第 3 阶段数据（n= 2,504），并且可以免费访问

介绍

“These pipelines facilitate the analysis of mtDNA data, but can be challenging to install.” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 64) 这些管道有助于 mtDNA 数据的分析，但安装起来可能具有挑战性。“MitoBamAnnotator：一种基于网络的工具，用于检测和注释人类线粒体 DNA 序列中的异质性,mit-o-matic：用于临床评估下一代测序数据集线粒体变异的综合计算管道but they were limited to small input files, revealed shortcomings in usability, overloaded with parameter options, or generate poor and often unreliable results” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 64) 但它们仅限于较小的输入文件，暴露出可用性方面的缺陷，参数选项过多，或者生成较差且通常不可靠的结果。

讨论

“The validation of the integrated heteroplasmy approach shows that mtDNAServer detects heteroplasmic variants and sample contamination accurately.” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 68) 集成异质性方法的验证表明，mtDNAServer 可以准确检测异质性变异和样品污染。

“mtDNAServer is conceived to handle large amount of mtDNA NGS data, by taking advantage of parallel data processing using the MapReduce framework, having the main focus on ease of use and reliability of the results to prevent erroneous conclusions.” (Weissensteiner 等, 2016, p. 68) mtDNAServer旨在通过利用MapReduce框架的并行数据处理来处理大量mtDNA NGS数据，主要关注结果的易用性和可靠性，以防止错误的结论。
