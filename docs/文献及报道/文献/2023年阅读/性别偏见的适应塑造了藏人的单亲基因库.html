性别偏见的适应塑造了藏人的单亲基因库

Sex-biased adaptation shapes uniparental gene pools in Tibetans

“multiple dispersals into theTibetan Plateau, which canbe briefly assigned intotwoepisodes (” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 多次扩散到青藏高原，可以简单地分为两段：

  1. “The ancient dispersal occurring during theUpper Paleolithic, even before thelast glacial maximum (LGM, 25–19 thousand years ago, ka), was traced back totheearly Eurasian Homo sapiens” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 古代的扩散发生在旧石器时代晚期，甚至在末次盛冰期（LGM）之前，可以追溯到早期的欧亚智人接收来自古老丹尼索瓦人的基因流。
  2. “The recent expansion within themidHolocene was ascribed totheNeolithic farmers andherders mainly derived from northern China” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 全新世中期最近的扩张被归因于主要来自中国北方的新石器时代农牧民。

“The dissection ofuniparental markers reveals distinct patterns ofYchromosome andmtDNA profiles inmodern Tibetans. Theextremely ancient Ychromosome lineage,haplogroup D-M174 with ageestimationbefore theLGM ((32.5±11.5) ka),was prevalent (54.3%) inTibetans” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 对单亲标记的剖析揭示了现代藏人 Y 染色体和 mtDNA 谱的独特模式。极其古老的Y染色体谱系，单倍群D-M174，年龄估计在末次盛世之前（32.5±11.5 ka），在藏族人中普遍存在（54.3％）。

“Incontrast, themost ancient indigenous mtDNA lineage, haplogroup M62 ((27.7±10.2) ka),existed with alow frequency of~2% (Zhao etal.,2009; Qiet al.,2013).” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 相比之下，最古老的本土线粒体DNA谱系，单倍群M62（（27.7±10.2）ka），以约2%的较低频率存在。

“The maternal gene pool of modern Tibetans was substantially shaped bytheNeolithic expansion of millet farmers from thelow-latitude Yellow River basin ofnorthern China, as characterized bytheprevalence ofmtDNA haplogroups such asM9a1a1c1b1a (5.9–3.7 ka)and A11a1a (10.2–3.3 ka)” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 现代藏人的母系基因库在很大程度上是由来自中国北方低纬度黄河流域的粟农的新石器时代扩张所塑造的，其特征是线粒体DNA单倍群的普遍存在，如M9a1a1c1b1a（5.9-3.7 ka）和A11a1a（10.2-3.3 ka）。

“modern Tibetans ischaracterized bythe lineages from Upper Paleolithic male hunter-gatherers from thehighlands and Neolithic female farmers from thelowlands” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 现代藏人的血统是旧石器时代晚期来自高地的男性狩猎采集者和新石器时代来自低地的女性农民的血统。

“This sex-biased pattern has not been explained yet.” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 令人费解的是，现代藏人拥有如此之多（>50%）的旧石器时代晚期父系血统，尤其是接受了新石器时代农业学家的广泛遗传贡献。这种性别偏见模式尚未得到解释。

“high- and low-altitudes presented anatlas ofthe adaptive traits innative Tibetans, in which some showed sex-biased phenotypicdifferences (” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 高海拔和低海拔地区呈现出藏族本土适应性特征的图谱，其中一些表现出性别偏向的表型差异。

“Inthe Tibetan samples, themonthly andseasonalchanges inbirth weight were more obvious inmale than infemale fetuses” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 1) 西藏样本中，男性胎儿出生体重的月度和季节变化较女性更为明显。

“themale fetuses were more sensitive tohypoxia than the female fetuses.” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 2) 男性胎儿比女性胎儿对缺氧更敏感。

“Evidently, thenative Tibetan male fetuses have higher fitness under hypoxia than theHan Chinese male fetuses.” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 2) 可见，藏族男胎在缺氧条件下的适应度高于汉族男胎。

“integrating thesex-biased patterns existingintheanalysis oftheuniparental gene pool, phenome, and gene expression to update the population history inthe Tibetan Plateau” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 2) 整合单亲基因库、表型和基因表达分析中存在的这些性别偏见模式，以更新青藏高原的人口历史

“headaptive introgression ofthe Denisovan-like EPAS1 gene which contributes toreproductive fitness (Heetal.,2023a; Yue et al.,2023) occurred during theUpper Paleolithic (Zhang etal.,2021).” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 2) 有助于生殖健康的类丹尼索瓦人 EPAS1 基因的适应性渗入发生在旧石器时代晚期（Zhang et al.,2021）。

“ncient hunter-gatherers living inhigh altitudes, such as males with Ychromosome haplogroup D-M174, harbored theadaptive EPAS1 haplotype.” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 2) 生活在高海拔地区的古代狩猎采集者，例如具有 Y 染色体单倍群 D-M174 的男性，拥有适应性 EPAS1 单倍型。

“After admixture with the Neolithic farmers and herders from low altitudes, theadmixed population migrated across theTibetan Plateau.” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 2) 与新石器时代低海拔地区的农牧民混合后，这些混杂人口跨越青藏高原迁徙。

“The stronger selective pressure duetohypoxia onmale fetuses likely maintained the docents with Upper Paleolithic lineage such ashaplogroup D-M174, asthey might have high-altitude adaptative EPAS1 gene.” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 2) 男性胎儿因缺氧而产生的更强的选择压力可能保留了旧石器时代晚期谱系，例如单倍群D-M174，因为他们可能具有高海拔适应EPAS1基因。

“Incontrast, theselection of hypoxia was relatively weak infemale fetuses, which might allow theexpansion ofNeolithic maternal lineages such as haplogroups M9a1a1c1b1a andA11a1a from thelowlands” (Peng 和 Zhang, p. 2) 相比之下，女性胎儿的缺氧选择相对较弱，这可能允许新石器时代母系谱系的扩展，例如来自低地的单倍群M9a1a1c1b1a和A11a1a。
