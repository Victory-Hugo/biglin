摘要

mit-o-matic: a comprehensive computational pipeline for clinical evaluation of mitochondrial variations from next-generation sequencing datasets

“The pipeline is made available as a user-friendly online tool to annotate variants and find haplogroup, disease association and heteroplasmic sites.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 2) 该管道作为用户友好的在线工具提供，用于注释变异并查找单倍群、疾病关联和异质位点。“To the best of our knowledge, this is the first and the most comprehensive tool for clinical evaluation of mitochondrial genomic variations from Next Generation Sequencing datasets. The tool is freely available at http://genome.igib.res.in/mitomatic/.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 2) 据我们所知，这是用于临床评估下一代测序数据集线粒体基因组变异的第一个也是最全面的工具。该工具可在 http://genome.igib.res.in/mitomatic/ 上免费获取。

介绍

“MitoSeek (Guo, et al., 2013) is one such tool for NGS data analysis from mtDNA, captured as a by-product of whole-exome or whole-genome sequencing. Although, a very expedient resource, it offers just a command-line version that works specifically on large abundance of paired-end sequencing data and does not support single-end data.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 4) MitoSeek：提取线粒体信息并进行高通量线粒体测序分析(Guo, et al., 2013) 是一种用于从 mtDNA 进行 NGS 数据分析的工具，mtDNA 是全外显子组或全基因组测序的副产品。尽管这是一个非常方便的资源，但它仅提供一个命令行版本，专门用于大量的双端测序数据，并且不支持单端数据。“It reports heteroplasmy, somatic mutations, structural variance, relative mtDNA copy number and annotations for amino acid changes, without any clinical correlation of the findings. To this end, mit-o-matic has been created to provide most comprehensive annotation with relevant clinical association that includes haplogroup classification.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 4) 它报告了异质性、体细胞突变、结构变异、相对线粒体DNA拷贝数和氨基酸变化注释，但结果没有任何临床相关性。为此，创建了 mit-o-matic，以提供最全面的注释以及相关的临床关联，包括单倍群分类。

“To the best of our knowledge, mit-o-matic is to date one of the most comprehensive analysis pipelines for clinical annotation of mitochondrial sequencing data from Next Generation Sequencing platforms.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 5) 据我们所知，mit-o-matic 是迄今为止最全面的分析管道之一，用于对下一代测序平台的线粒体测序数据进行临床注释。

“It provides automation for alignment of NGS reads to the reference mitochondrial genome, fetching the variants above a set cut-off and creating a descriptive annotation of reported variants.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 5) 它提供了将 NGS 读数与参考线粒体基因组比对的自动化功能，获取高于设定截止值的变异并创建报告变异的描述性注释。“The web based interface can accept data in form of FASTQ files directly and perform the analysis entirely online (Option 1), but with a size limitation, which allows an ample coverage of approximately 500x of the mitochondrial genome which is more than sufficient for most clinical applications.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 5) 基于网络的界面可以直接接受 FASTQ 文件形式的数据并完全在线执行分析（选项 1），但有大小限制，可以充分覆盖约 500 X的线粒体基因组，这对于大多数临床来说绰绰有余应用程序。“For large datasets above 500x coverage as in very specialized applications, the command-line option can be used for either running the complete pipeline or just parsing an already available pileup file.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 5) 对于覆盖率超过 500 倍的大型数据集（如非常专业的应用程序），命令行选项可用于运行完整的管道或仅解析已可用的堆积文件。

“For each variant, the reference allele, alternate allele, percentage of allele frequency, corresponding gene, amino acid position, and amino acid change with its subsequent effect (Synonymous or Non-synonymous) are listed as per the revised Cambridge Reference Sequence” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 5) 对于每个变体，参考等位基因、替代等位基因、等位基因频率百分比、相应基因、氨基酸位置和氨基酸变化及其后续影响（同义或非同义）按照修订后的剑桥参考序列列出“variants are reported with associated phenotype, population frequencies are reported for variants that are not novel and potential functional consequence is informed for nonsynonymous novel variants.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 6) 报告变异与相关表型，报告非新颖变异的群体频率，并告知非同义新颖变异的潜在功能后果。“It provides an online user-friendly graphical user interface where the researcher or clinician can avail a comprehensive genetic report on the mitochondrial variations in a dataset.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 6) 它提供了一个在线用户友好的图形用户界面，研究人员或临床医生可以利用该界面获取有关数据集中线粒体变异的全面遗传报告。

结论

“which could start from mitochondrial sequences, and perform assembly, annotation and evaluation of clinically relevant variations.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 11) 它可以从线粒体序列开始，对临床相关变异进行组装、注释和评估。“It can support any kind of high quality single-end or paired-end data that has been mapped to the mitochondrial genome, including amplicon sequencing, whole genome sequencing and custom enrichment.” (Vellarikkal 等, 2015, p. 11) 它可以支持已映射到线粒体基因组的任何类型的高质量单端或双端数据，包括扩增子测序、全基因组测序和定制富集。
